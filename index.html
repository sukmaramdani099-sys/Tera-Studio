Our Story

TERA STUDIO

KÄLLA translates directly from Swedish as "The Source." Conceived in Stockholm and hand-crafted meticulously with luxury materials, KÄLLA is built on raw architectural minimalism, absolute monochromatic discipline, and silent structural presence.

We operate in contrast to modern hyper-fast retail systems. Our drops are small, deliberate collections featuring raw structural tailoring, concrete-grey hues, dark-cement backdrops, and seamless tactile aesthetics. We prioritize visual longevity and physical resilience in all elements.
